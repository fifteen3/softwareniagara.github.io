Software Niagara is a grassroots initiative to build a stronger software community and startup culture in the Niagara Region.

We are a group of local designers, developers, entrepreneurs, and students who believe that Niagara is a great place to create new technology and grow businesses. We believe that sharing, collaboration, and community spirit are vital to building a strong technology sector in Niagara.

We are trying to make Niagara a great place to live and work for designers, developers, and entrepreneurs by:

  • Organizing DemoCamp Niagara to showcase "Made in Niagara" technology
  • Organizing hackathons with a focus on open data and civic engagement
  • Operating #softwareniagara on IRC
  • Promoting Coworking and Startup Drinks
  • Promoting local startup and technology groups and events
  • Encouraging children and adults to develop computer literacy and programming skills

History

We started in 2012 with the goal of organizing technology and startup events (with a focus on software) to build a community that fostered innovation and excellence in Niagara. The inspiration for our grassroots effort came from Software Hamilton and Startup North, both of which were similar efforts that had been instrumental in building vibrant tech and startup communities in Hamilton and Toronto, respectively.

Structure

Software Niagara is a grassroots group. There is no official mandate, nor are there any affiliations with business, government, or non-government organizations. All work is carried out by volunteers acting on behalf of themselves. Any sponsorship funds are used solely to cover event costs.
