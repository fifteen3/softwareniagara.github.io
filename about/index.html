Software Niagara is a grassroots initiative to build a stronger software community and startup culture in the Niagara Region.

Software Niagara is organized by local designers, developers, entrepreneurs, and students. All of whom believe that Niagara is a great place to create new technology and grow businesses. Furthermore, these individuals believe that sharing, collaboration, and community spirit are vital to building a strong technology sector in Niagara.

Software Niagara is trying to make Niagara a great place to live and work for designers, developers, and entrepreneurs by:

  • Organizing DemoCamp Niagara to showcase "Made in Niagara" technology
  • Organizing hackathons with a focus on open data and civic engagement
  • Operating #softwareniagara on IRC to facilitate knowledge exchange
  • Promoting coworking and Startup Drinks
  • Promoting local startup and technology groups and events
  • Encouraging children and adults to develop computer literacy and programming skills

Our History

Software Niagara was founded in 2012 based on the Software Hamilton concept. Software Hamilton played a major role in building a vibrant technology and startup community in Hamilton, Ontario.

Our Structure

Software Niagara is a grassroots group. There is no official mandate, nor are there any affiliations with business, government, or non-governmental organizations. All work is carried out by volunteers. Sponsorship is used solely to cover event costs.
